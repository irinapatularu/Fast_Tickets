Who We Are

FastTickets is an online platform where you can buy tickets faster for events.

There are a lot of social, cultural or artistic events where only certain people can participate. These events are organized by highschool or university students and the tickets can be bought only from them. We are coming in aid by providing an application where people can buy tickets easily and organizers can track their availability.

What We Do

We have everything you need to organize an event.

Our services include a web application for organizers and participants to sell or buy event tickets and also a mobile application in order to validate those tickets.
